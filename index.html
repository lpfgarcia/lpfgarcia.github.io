About

Luís Paulo Faina Garcia received the B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering in 2010, from University of São Paulo, Brazil. He is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Computer Sciences and Mathematics, University of São Paulo, Brazil. His current research interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, Meta-learning and Data Preprocessing.
