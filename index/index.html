Luís Paulo F. Garcia

He is a postdoctoral researcher at the Smart Data Analysis (SDA) Laboratory at Leipzig University. His research interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, Meta-learning and Data Preprocessing.

Email: garcia [at] informatik [dot] uni-leipzig [dot] de

About

Luís Paulo Faina Garcia received the B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering in 2010 and the Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2016 from University of São Paulo, Brazil. During the Ph.D. he performed two internships, one at University of Granada and another at Dalhousie University. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Smart Data Analysis Laboratory at Leipzig University, Germany. His current research interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, Data Streams, Meta-learning and Knowledge Graphs.
