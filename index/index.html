About

Luís Paulo Faina Garcia received the BSc. degree in Computer Engineering in 2010 and the Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2016 from University of São Paulo, Brazil. In 2017, he received two awards of best thesis by the Brazilian Government Council (CAPES) and Brazilian Computer Society (SBC). He is currently a professor in the Computer Science Department - University of Brasilia, Brazil. His research interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, Data Streams and Meta-learning.
